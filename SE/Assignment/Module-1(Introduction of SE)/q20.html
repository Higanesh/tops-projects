20. What is Flow chart ? Create a flowchart to make addition of two numbers ? -> A flowchart is a diagram that represents a process or algorithm. It uses various symbols to denote different types of actions or steps, and arrows to show the flow of control from one step to another. Flowcharts are used for planning, documenting, analyzing, and designing processes. Common Flowchart Symbols: 1. Oval: Indicates the start and end of a process. 2. Rectangle: Represents a process or operation. 3. Diamond: Denotes a decision point. 4. Parallelogram: Represents input/output operations. 5. Arrow: Shows the direction of the flow of the process.
